WFC can be accessed in most WDG games, to access then press COMPNET WFC on the title screen. then it will open the COMPNET Menu,

First you need to make a COMPNET Account, you will choose a username and a password, you will use that account to connect online. Then the software will try to connect to the internet and receive data from the COMPNET Server or a private server, check the @deletedtapes youtube channel to find out info about how to make a custom server.
